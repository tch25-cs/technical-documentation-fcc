Threat Model

Identify assets, actors, entry points, and abuse cases. Consider STRIDE and MITRE ATT&CK mappings.

Asset: Authentication tokens
Threat: Token theft via XSS or logs
Impact: High
Mitigation: HttpOnly cookies, CSP, secret vaulting

Controls

Map controls to frameworks (e.g., NIST CSF / SP 800-53). Keep them testable and outcome-driven.

  • AC-2 Account Management
  • SC-13 Cryptographic Protection
  • AU-6 Audit Review, Analysis, and Reporting

Implementation

Step-by-step tasks, configuration snippets, and validation commands.

# Example: enforce TLS 1.2+ on nginx
ssl_protocols TLSv1.2 TLSv1.3;
ssl_ciphers HIGH:!aNULL:!MD5;
Warning: Validate cipher compatibility before rollout to avoid client breakage.
